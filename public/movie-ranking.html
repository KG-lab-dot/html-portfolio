Best Movies of All Time, according to Kgotso

My top 3 movies are:

1. Rocky

Rocky tugs on my heartstrings with its underdog story and Adrian is the sweetest character ever. It's a classic that never gets old. I love how it captures the spirit of persveraance and triumph over adversity. The training montages are iconic, I love the music and I literally use the Rocky soundtracks when I train myself, lol.

2. The Godfather

I love the Godfathers complex characters and the way it explores themes of power, family, and loyalty. The cinematography is stunning, and the performances are stellar. It also has a beautiful soundtracks and that is really important to me as I am a music head. Family and loyalty are thems that resonate with me deeply.

3. Eternal Sunshine of the Spotless Mind

I watched this movie maybe twice, I think I will visit it tonight lol. But it has stuck with me emotionally. Although I do not remember the plot, I remember the feeling it left me with. The soundtrack is also amazing. A great rapper by the name of Jay Electronica sampled the soundtrack for one of his projects and it is a piece of artistic gold.
